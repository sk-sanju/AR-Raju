About Me.

I am Raju, the owner of AR Handlooms based in Kuthampully, a village renowned for its traditional Kerala handloom heritage. With years of experience in textile craftsmanship and business management, I am dedicated to preserving authentic weaving traditions while bringing elegant handwoven products to modern customers.

My passion lies in supporting local artisans, ensuring premium quality products, and promoting Kerala’s cultural identity through traditional sarees such as Kasavu, Set Mundu, and wedding collections. I believe in combining tradition with innovation to meet evolving customer needs.
